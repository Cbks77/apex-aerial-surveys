03 — The Process

How It Works

Three simple steps from your first call to a professional report in your inbox.

01

Book & Brief

Tell us about your site, what you need inspected and your timeline. We'll confirm availability, check airspace, and agree a scope of work — usually within 24 hours.

02

We Fly

Our CAA-licensed pilot attends site, conducts the flight safely and captures all required imagery and data. Typically 1–3 hours on site depending on scope.

03

Report Delivered

You receive a professional PDF inspection report — annotated images, key findings, recommendations and all raw footage — within 24 hours of the visit.
